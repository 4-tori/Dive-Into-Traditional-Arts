About Me











Hi! I am Trisha,




A third year student at the University of Caloocan City, studying for a Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (BSEMC).
I created this website to give aspiring artists a quick overview of traditional arts, such as drawing and painting.
Having started with traditional drawing before moving into digital art, I find this journey both nostalgic and enjoyable.
I've gathered some of the tutorials I read and watched during my learning process.


On this section, I will showcase my works in both traditional and digital art forms.
I hope to share my journey and inspire fellow artists. I aim for this website to be a valuable resource
and a source of inspiration for anyone exploring the world of art.
